Important because the 2026 format has variable third-place qualifiers.

How the checker decides stages
  • Same group: the teams are guaranteed to meet in the group stage.
  • Different groups: the checker tests every published Round of 32 slot, then follows the bracket tree through Round of 16, quarterfinals, semifinals, and final.
  • Third-place entries are conditional because only eight of twelve third-place teams advance, and their exact Round of 32 placement depends on the final set of qualifying third-place groups.
  • "Possible" means a bracket path exists. It does not predict that either team will qualify or win matches.
